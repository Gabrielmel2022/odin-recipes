Ingredients Hamburgers !

  1. 1 pound of ground beef
  2. Hamburger buns
  3. Pepper
  4. Ketchup
  5. Mustard/li>
  6. Cheese
  7. Salt

Steps For Making Hamburgers

  1. Mush your ground beef for 2 min
  2. Cook on grill for 20 min
  3. Cook to well-done or your liking
  4. Flip burgers as neccessary
  5. Place patties in buns with condiments on top
  6. Smush down to gather hamburger into shape
  7. Enjoy!
