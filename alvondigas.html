Alvodigas Recipes!

Alvondigas is a Mexican soup that has been enjoyed around the world for centuries. It stems from the North of Mexico in the sonoran regions. It is a staple for the winter months as well as when you are feeling sick or hungover. It is a very lean healthy meal to enjoy with its carrots, squash, cucumber, and broth. During winter time you can never go wrong with a nice bowl of alvondigas. It is very popular in California and throughout the southwest. Enjoy yourself a nice easy to make bowl!

Ingredients for Alvondigas !

  1. ground beef
  2. water
  3. Pepper
  4. Cilantro
  5. carrots
  6. cucumber

Steps For Making Alvondigas

  1. Pour a bowl of water into pot
  2. Roll meatballs into shape and season with pepper and salt
  3. Heat bowl to medium
  4. Let cook for 20 minutes
  5. Continue stirring
  6. Look for cooked meatballs by piercing
  7. Turn off heat and let cool down
  8. Enjoy!
